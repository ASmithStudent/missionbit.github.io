Volunteers

Classroom volunteer

Classroom Volunteers play an essential role by providing one on one support to our students and assisting our instructors. With an understanding of the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, java, and javascript, you will engage a diverse group of students through a project based coding course.

One-Time event volunteers

One-Time Events Volunteer support the Mission Bit staff with activities throughout the year. Events typically occur outside of the classroom, at various locations throughout San Francisco and Oakland (e.g. Demo Day, Mission Bit Gala, Student Admissions Interview, Workshops).
