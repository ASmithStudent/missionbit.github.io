Free Coding Classes for Public School Students in San Francisco, CA

Spring 2014 Students

First things first, you should come to the Orientation / Welcome Party this THURSDAY, JANUARY 30, 4-5:30PM. It's okay if you can't make it to Orientation, but we highly recommend it. Below are some class details, but we'll send you additional details, via email, Thursday evening after the Orientation.

Start dates:

  • Intermediate – 4pm Monday, February 3rd
  • Introduction – 4pm Tuesday, February 4th

Mission Bit partners with another non-profit Out of Site to offer the Intro and Intermediate Web Programming classes. The orientation is being hosted at Out of Site's offices. Out of Site is located at 29 Howth Street, between Ocean & Geneva, near Balboa BART and MUNI J line. Look for Out of Site's infamous purple front door! If you have any trouble finding Out of Site you can call them at (415) 574-8137.

Note: If you are a Mission High School student your classes (but not the orientation) will be held at MHS, but it is still recommended that you attend the orientation at Out of Site.

At the Orientation, you'll:

  • Get more details about the Mission Bit class
  • Have a chance to meet some Mission Bit instructors
  • Begin to brainstorm the cool apps you want to build this semester
  • Meet our partners at Out of Site, learn about their other exciting programs and meet the students in the other Out of Site classes
  • Pick two tech companies you'd like to visit this semester
  • Enjoy some delicious treats!

Please use this form to confirm you will be able to attend the orientation. http://bit.ly/mBitintro

We look forward to seeing you at the Orientation and having a great semester together.

In the meantime feel free to reach us via phone, (415) 519-4056, or email info@missionbit.com.
