Mobile Game Development Class

Another program we are running this summer is a free immersive (6 week, 5 days/week, 7 hours/day) mobile game development class for 12 SF public high school students this summer. The class started on June 6th and ends on July 11. After two weeks of work on game design best practices, dev projects on sample mobile games and java tutorials, the class has now been split into 3 teams. By the end of the class, the students will have an Android game ready for the Google Play Store. The class has featured guest lecturers from SF mobile game companies such as Zynga, DeNA West, Glu, Chartboost and Pocket Gems.

Wolf Pack


Team Members:
Monty Choy
Kelly Huang
Herman Lin
Chris Lim


Story Fang is one of the wolves in his wolf pack, and he was bullied by other members of the pack. He must go through the trial of wolves in order to strengthen his ability and prove himself to his comrades. He must take the trial to go from pup to a mature, powerful wolf. There are monstrous creatures on his road of trial. In the end, he proves himself worthy and becomes the very best like no one ever was.
Mechanics Hold the left side of the screen to move back. Hold the right side of the screen to move forward. Tap the enemy to automatically move to a reasonable range, and attack the enemy once. Swipe left to go back swiftly (in order to dodge enemy attack). Swipe right to dash forward (needs upgrade. Tap the player itself to activate defense (reduce damage). Open upgrade menu by pressing menu located on the upper right corner, which enables player to upgrade the character’s skills.

Pro-taters


Team Members:
Ryan Hu
Tommy Wong
Susan Tham

Story You’re a magician whose sole duty is to protect your village from hordes and hordes of monsters who are trying to steal your village’s taters (potatoes) in other to make tater tots. As the magician you can never “die” however monsters can attack you and if they bring your HP down to 0 you “die”, but are later revived at your village, well and healthy. However if the village is destroyed, then the game is lost.
There are about 16 levels you can play, or you can play the endless Sandbox mode to challenge yourself to an infinite horde. As you beat levels you have the chance to upgrade your magician and/or village to help you and your village to last against the horde, using your spoils of war (gold) and points which can be converted to your magician’s EXP or your magician’s skills.
Mechanics Waves of monsters will spawn during a level, depending on how far you have progressed monsters will either get tougher, increase in number, or both. You must defend your village from the monsters using the magician you control. As the magician you will have a basic attack, skills, and items. Attacks and skills can be upgraded, while items can be bought or picked up as monster drops. You can move left or right to either fall down to the village or engage any enemies. Your village has HP, if that HP reaches zero, you lose. Monsters will gradually bring down your village’s HP when they reach your village. If your village is still standing at the end of the level you win that level and gain points and gold from the level, which is spent on items and/or upgrades.
http://www.missionbit.com/projects/Pro-taters
