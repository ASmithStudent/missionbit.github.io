Mobile Game Development Class


Another program we are running this summer is a free immersive (6 week, 5 days/week, 7 hours/day) mobile game development class for 12 SF public high school students this summer. The class started on June 6th and ends on July 11. After two weeks of work on game design best practices, dev projects on sample mobile games and java tutorials, the class has now been split into 3 teams. By the end of the class, the students will have a 'play store' ready game. The class has featured guest lecturers from SF mobile game companies such as Zynga, DeNA West, Glu, Chartboost and Pocket Gems.

Swipe RPG


Team Members Ryan Hu Tommy Wong Susan Tham
StoryIn a fantasy world where magic and monsters exist, humans are known as the weakest lifeforms on the planet because they cannot use magic. The main character is an orphan and one of the only few humans who can use magic because his/her father infused his own blood with dragon blood, giving his child(you/main character) magical abilities. However humans with magical abilities are considered evil, as many use their magic for their own personal gain(greed, power, etc.) and are called Endonin or Endors for short. There are eight races living on this planet. Humans, Winoas, Dragons, -to be continued later-
(Note: ‘you’ will reference the player from now on). One day your village is attacked by dragons while you are out fishing, you come back to the sight of your village engulfed in flames. 1st Quest saving people at your orphanage. After saving them a dragon comes back to attack you but without knowing you cast magic, which makes the dragon kidnap you to its mountain lair nearby. 2nd Quest learn from the dragon(Tutorial). Mechanics Main character has a sword and gun he/she dual wields. Gun is used by pressing a button, swordplay is used by swiping/tapping the screen that takes player taps and swipes. Movement is done with buttons.
