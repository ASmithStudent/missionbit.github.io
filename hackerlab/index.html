A hacker space for public high school students to explore, learn and hack with volunteer engineers and entreprenuers

The Hacker Lab

@ Mission High School, Room 404

Support The Hacker Lab

The Hacker Lab is a modern interpretation of a public high school computer lab with three primary goals.

The Hacker Lab will be a place where SF public school students, with support from Mission Bit volunteer mentors, can get help with personal projects, experiment with new technologies and explore APIs for cool tech like Google Glass, Leap Motion

Via the Hacker Lab, Mission Bit will collaborate with instructors in the math, science and art departments at Mission High School to develop ‘blended learning’ lessons and projects

The energy, activity and cool tech in the Hacker Lab will hopefully attract the interest of students that have prematurely identified themselves as ‘non-coders’ to at least give it a shot.
