DEMO DAY

Imagine stepping into a coding class with ZERO experience and an unquenchable thirst for knowledge. Now, imagine yourself just 60 hours later with a website and an arcade game that you created all on your own!

This is the story of every new student at Mission Bit. And this is exactly why, every semester, we open our doors to share the successes and incredible journeys these SF Bay students embark on.

Demo day is the perfect time to connect with your community and meet the next generation of web developers, software engineers, game designers, and entrepreneurs.

What to expect:

  • Absolutely delicious food
  • Live demos of student projects
  • An incredible group of like-minded individuals who are invested in the future of education
We hope to see you there!
