Hack-A-Bit

Friday, July 13th at USF - Harney Science Center

Join us for a day of hacking! Through our efforts, we seek to encourage students to become aware of and take part in the growing technology field. This event will include different workshops as well as the creation of a prototype, by each team, of their own custom learning management system. We believe this hackathon will encourage students to actively participate in designing their future.
